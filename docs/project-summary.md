**The Problem: Beyond the “Google Panic”**  
For most people, the immediate reaction to physical discomfort is “Google Panic”—a frantic search that often yields worst-case scenarios, misinformation, and unnecessary health stress. While some healthcare providers offer credible articles, these are static and cannot contextualize symptoms or engage in dialogue. Furthermore, for adolescents or uninsured individuals, the barrier to care is high due to intimidation, cost, or lack of guidance. Current AI tools often lack credibility, leaving users stuck deciding if a scary search result warrants an intimidating doctor's visit. 

**Our Solution: Providing a Bridge to Confident Care**  
Our app addresses this gap by providing a friendly, interactive AI companion designed to help users contextualize their health. Instead of providing a singular diagnosis, the app contains many disclaimers and identifies the three to five most likely causes for symptoms. We frame these as “conversation starters” that can be taken directly to a physician. By focusing on transparency and clinical preparation, we aim to help users transition from stressed and confused to empowered with a plan. Once a user is done with a particular conversation, they have the option to delete it for increased privacy and less clutter. 

**Current Features Synthesized:**

* **Safety-First AI Consultation:** A natural language interface where users describe symptoms in plain English. The AI asks clarifying follow-up questions (severity, duration, sensation) to provide nuanced insights. This excludes information saved to a user’s profile, which is automatically considered.   
* **Recovery Insights:** The app provides probable causes and non-medication comfort measures (hydration, dark room) while strictly adhering to safety disclaimers and emergency redirects.   
* **History Accessibility:** The app includes a chat history sidebar, in which past conversations are chronologically ordered and deletable. It is also collapsible for users wanting additional privacy but not yet done with past insights. This will tie into features yet to be implemented, such as personalized check-in notifications and daily wellness streaks. 

**Future Feature Goals:**

* **The “Physician Bridge”:** A “Yelp for healthcare” system, starting with campus-specific health centers. This allows users to review wait times, bedside manner, and costs, helping them feel confident in where they seek care. We are planning to keep responses anonymous.   
* **Clinical Summaries:** The ability to synthesize chat history into a concise report of symptoms, severity, and duration, resulting in a tangible document to reduce the “intimidation factor” during appointments.  
* **Proactive Health Companion:** A three-tiered reminder system for medical-follow ups. Appointment logistics, and daily wellness “streaks” to support users even when they aren’t in crisis.
